About Us

Inspired from various attractive student learning websites, we decided to create our very own website for CSE students. During these tough time, we know everyone is trying to keep things as normal as they can. to do our bit we are launching this website for students doing engineering in any year.

The goal of this website is to give the students as much exposure and knowelege. We are aware that this lockdown can't replace the human interaction. Especially the one with a teacher and student. The main objective of this website is to provide all knowledge on these three subjects- DSA, JAVA and OS

It contains notes, videos, pdf etc for students to study.

Objectives

  1. To build an user-friendly global online platform to help parents find free resources appropriate for their children's needs.
  2. To create a website that makes learning more interesting and easy for the students during this pandemic situation.
  3. To provide supplementary videos, e-books or articles and course modules to the students in order to provide tthem with sufficient knowledge required in the course selected by them.
